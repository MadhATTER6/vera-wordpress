Get Involved

GET INVOLVED / VOLUNTEER

Want to be a Vera volunteer?
Vera is a true to life ‘choose your own adventure’ story – you can run shows, help in the silkscreen studio or recording studio, curate exhibitions in the gallery… anything you want really.

STEP ONE:
GO TO A VOLUNTEER ORIENTATION (required)
Learn about Vera’s history and all of our different programs and services and meet other volunteers that you will be working with. Our Steering committee members who lead these orientations can help you answer all of your ‘what’s next?’ questions to get you on the right path.
